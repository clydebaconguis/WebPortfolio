Internship Reflection

We started our journey at Fligno with introduction of our SRE supervisor Mr. Kev a very well respected and hardworking leader. He is assign in teaching us the field of Site Reliability Engineering.


Not wasting time we started learning Linux fundamentals and installing necessary softwares like Vagrant, Virtual Boxes, Terraform, terrugrunt, vscode and etc. After learning we have been given tasks to perform and also we take challenges to truly grasp its usages.

I am so grateful because I was given the chance to learn basic Laravel crud api and Reactjs. I was able to fully grasp the roles of backend and frontend development. Fligno truly gave opportunities to those students who are willing to learn in IT industry and I'm the proof of their services. Thank you once again Fligno and Teams, See you around.
